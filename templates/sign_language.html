Gesture → Output Reference

Use these hand gestures for the corresponding detected words. Ensure your hand is clearly visible in the frame.

✋ Hand Gesture (Sign) Output Word
HELLO — Wave hand side to side (fingers together, palm out) HELLO
YES — Nodding fist: closed fist, move hand up and down (like nodding). Not thumbs down. YES
NO — Index and middle finger together, tap or twist at chin NO
THANK YOU — Flat hand from chin forward (fingers toward lips, then move out) THANK YOU
PLEASE — Flat hand on chest, circular rub PLEASE
Predictions use hand pose (finger count and hand position) when a hand is detected; otherwise a fallback is used. For YES use a nodding fist (fist moving up/down), not thumbs down.
